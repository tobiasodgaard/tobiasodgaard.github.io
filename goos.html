GoOS

Course Project for Playable Media, IT University, Copenhagen 2019
Try GoOS!

A simulation of an operating system where all elements are physics based. Pick (up) your user name, help the system load and then listen to music, read email and check the time, while all the windows bounces around your desktop.

The purpose of GoOS was to challenge the conventional operating system by factoring in the element of play.

Specifications: Developed in Unity

Roles: Gameplay programmer & Game designer

Development Team: Rasmus Emil Odgaard, Nikolaj Schildt Christensen & Jacob Hvid Amstrup
