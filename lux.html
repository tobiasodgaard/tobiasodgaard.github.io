Lux

Semester project, B.Sc. Medialogy, Aalborg University, Copenhagen 2018

Lux is a first-person exploration game. Set in a dark dungeon you are equipped with a glowing orb to help guide you. Throw the orb to light the way and activate hidden contraptions.

The main purpose of Lux was to investigate player behaviour by using dynamic difficulty.

Specifications: Developed in Unity

Roles: Gameplay programmer, Game designer

Development Team: Rasmus Emil Odgaard, Nikolaj Schildt Christensen, Nikolaj Schlütter, Jakob Ranum, Sarah Ostenfeld Bitsch & Søren Dahl Poulsen
