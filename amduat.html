Amduat. A Play Machine

Company collaboration w. Hotel Pro Forma, 2019

Amduat. A play machine is an experimental 2D narrative experience, using only the scroll wheel as input device.

Guide Re through the underworld, exploring the 12 hours each with their own minigames, wrapped in cinematic visuals and sound design.

The game is developed in collaboration with Hotel Pro Forma as a companion piece for their 2021 play Amduat. An Oxygen Machine, and will be published as part of its marketing campaign early 2021.

Specifications: Developed in Unity

Roles: Gameplay programmer, Game designer & Prototyping
