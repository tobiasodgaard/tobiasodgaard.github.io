Pineapple

Small Team Game Development Project, Ongoing

Pineapple is a 2D strategic puzzle game for all ages. Guide the pineapple through a maze by programming his movement.

The twist is that your inputs are repeated, so planning ahead is recommended.

The game is still under development but demo can be downloaded here: itch-page

Specifications: Developed in Unity & FMOD

Roles: Game designer, tools and gameplay programmer

Development Team: Rasmus Emil Odgaard, John O'Donnell, Casper Boonen, Luis Fernando Laris Pardo & Bence Kovacs
