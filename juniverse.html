Juniverse

Bachelor Project, B.Sc. Medialogy, Aalborg University, Copenhagen 2018

Juniverse is a first-person exploration game, set on a cubical game-world. Depending on your route, the sides of the cube changes and allow you to visit a total of 16 different sides. Jump around and solve puzzles and collect unknown materials.

The main purpose of the project was to investigate curiosity of players and different play styles.

Specifications: Developed in Unity

Roles: Gameplay programmer, Game designer & Sound designer
